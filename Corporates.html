Trees are depleting in huge numbers and the future of the planet is at stake. Urbanization has created an imbalance in the environment. And we as society are responsible for it. Sustainability has become order of the day! To make up for the damage that is already done, SayTrees ensures that trees are planted every weekend. As you can imagine, the kind of support we need is huge.

Corporate tree planting projects can help create compelling conservation success stories and brand awareness for you. It can also increase staff engagement opportunities and add value to your environmental programmes. We, at SayTrees offer tree-planting partnerships for sustainability-minded companies.

Corporates who have partnered with us come from all sectors ranging from small companies to large corporations wishing to offset their carbon footprint. If you wish to join us, do write to us at info@citywoods
