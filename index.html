PLANTATION WITH FARMERS

CityWoods initiates plantation with farmers to support them with sustainable income. Farmers have been affected with the recurring droughts and uneven rain fall. Due to these odd scenario and the global climate changes, the farmers are facing lot of issues. And if this worsen then situation will be out of control. It is now agricultural dilemma. The farmers cannot stop the agriculture nor accept the losses caused by the recurring failures. To bring back the lost greenery we have been doing tree plantation in cities. But now we realize that by planting the same trees in villages, we can help the farmers have a sustainable income. We shall plant fruit bearing saplings with farmers and these trees will help them to have a parallel income.
